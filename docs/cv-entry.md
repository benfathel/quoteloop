## QuoteLoop - Full-Stack SaaS Project

- Built a mobile-first SaaS app for trade contractors to track quotes, automate follow-ups, and manage bookings.
- Developed the product with Next.js, TypeScript, Prisma, PostgreSQL, Tailwind CSS, NextAuth, Stripe, and n8n.
- Implemented quote workflows, subscription gating, customer intake forms, availability scheduling, booking actions, and notifications.
